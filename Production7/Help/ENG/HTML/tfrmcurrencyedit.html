Editing the currency settings

 Top  Previous  Next

Basically you enter the full and short names of the currency, keeping in mind that short name will be used almost everywhere and it is good habit to keep it short and distinct, not mentioning that the best is the conforming to the Three letter standard

The date is the beginning time from which this exchange rate will be in effect. The exchange rate record with next closest date will bound this from "from now to infinity" mode to simple "From this date to the next closest". This way you don't have to specify the end of the period ever.

The exchange rate in this example is set to 1:1 because the US dollars is set as the base or default currency.

For the living example of exchange rate settings you may study this screenshot to see that it is simple set by either EUR to USD rate or vice versa. In either case the opposite number will be automatically set to it's correct value.

And the last part is setting names as they will be seen in the printed forms or reports.

For the US Dollar you can place USD or just $ sign as "Whole" part and letter "c" or cent sign "¢" for the cents as fractional part
