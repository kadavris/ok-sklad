Goods balances log

 Top  Previous  Next

This report contains the information on all goods currently available at warehouse (all company warehouses). Selection criteria are defined by time period (from date to date), product groups and name of warehouse.

In addition, Goods balances log contains actual data on the quantity of available and reserved goods from total quantity available in stock. Information on this report is represented in quantities of goods as well as their value indicating average weighted price per unit.

You can start generating this report by clicking on icon at top toolbar or pressing «Ctrl + Р» or simply double-clicking on report name. A drop down window will allow you to define report selection criteria. You will need to specify the date for which the report is being generated or select it from the drop-down calendar, or enter it manually in the field.

Then you need to specify the name of the warehouse for which the report is being generated. You can select it from a drop-down menu or by clicking on icon. Press «Enter» to confirm your selection or double-click on the line with the warehouse name. If you are interested to get the report on all warehouses, select "All" in the warehouse field.

The next selection criterion is "product group" . You can filter through groups of products by selecting the required group from the drop-down window or by clicking on icon. Keep  in mind that in this case your selection is not limited to only product group. You have an option to generate a more detailed report on a sub-group, consecutively opening folders with the names of product groups until you define the required sub-group. Selection of product sub-group or group is done by clicking on "Select" button or just pressing "Enter". If you are interested in a short report on incoming products for all groups you may select "All" in this field

Generate by product groups    -   a check mark in this box will set or cancel generating report according to product groups. If you choose to generate the report, it will show product names in alphabetical order.

After you specify all parameters of the report, you need to click on "Preview"  or , which will bring up a document preview screen.

To print the document click on icon or press «Ctrl + Р».
