Main properties

It is simple and straightforward. Check if document is Posted, enter the date and time when the product becomes available at warehouse. Also you can change your document currency to the currency you used to purchase goods from suppliers. Do not forget to enter the exchange rate for proper price calculation for sales in  your default and other currency.

Specify the name of the warehouse where the goods are located.

 

Notes section
•Employee - your name here. If you have administrator privileges for this database, you can select any other employees.
•Reason - optional remark of why this document was created.

 

Items list

•No. - item sequence number
•Price VAT incl. - price of one unit of the product including VAT if applicable
•Amount in currency, USD - total in document's selected currency (USD in this example)
•Amount VAT incl. - total in the system-default currency including VAT if applicable.
