OK-Inventory is an innovative, versatile and customizable software package designed to provide inventory control, accounting and document management solutions to small and medium-sized trading and manufacturing companies.
The program interface is very simple and user-friendly. It will only take two or three days of training before you learn the key elements of the program and start using it for your business.

Main features include, but not limited to:

◆ Incoming goods or services from vendors and outgoing goods or services for your customers
◆ You have an option to sell items from specific lots
◆ Invoices and reservation of goods for customers
◆ Orders from customers or for vendors
◆ Goods returns from customers and vendors
◆ Price lists
◆ The program provides an option to assign various pricing categories to products
◆ Warranty slips for the items you sell        
◆ View your inventory at warehouses at one click
◆ View current finances and performance analysis at one click
◆ Search items by name, part number or barcode

 

ONLINE SALES:

◆ Upload your inventory to and download orders from your web site with one click of a mouse
◆ Customize the view of each product on web site including images
◆ Full set of functions for search engine optimization (Google, Yahoo, etc.)

 

FINANCES:

◆ Incoming payments
◆ Outgoing payments
◆ Additional costs
◆ Unlimited bank accounts and cash registers
◆ Multiple currency
◆ Balance adjustments on accounts
◆ Money transfers between accounts and withdrawal

 

WAREHOUSING AND INVENTORY MANAGEMENT:

◆ Unlimited warehouses and locations
◆ View current stock by one click
◆ View current product lots at warehouses
◆ Bar codes to work with inventory items
◆ Inventory
◆ Adjustments of goods balance

 

REPORTING:

•Sales and supplies
•Balances with suppliers and customers
•Profits and costs
•Profit of your sales persons
•and many more...

 

EMPLOYEE AND PROGRAM USER MANAGEMENT:

•Multi user system with personal login and password
•Users are able to view, change, create or delete each type of business document
•Restricted user access to specific warehouses
•You may restrict to view incoming prices for goods for specific user

 

OK-Inventory will help you streamline all business processes within your company.
