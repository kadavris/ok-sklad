A
B
Base currency - the main currency that you use everyday for your business activity. OK-Inventory supporting multiple currencies and helps you easy making business with foreign country business partners. The prices for the goods can be automatically converted from one currency to another supposing there is correct Exchange Rate is set in the currencies directory.
C
Cumulative Discount Card - Usually every purchase adds some small percentage of discount for the future purchases or accumulates bonus points of some kind that can be spent by the consumer later to his or her benefit
D
Discount Card - For large base of anonymous but regular customers it is a token that identifies this particular client to you with purchases patterns and amounts, but retaining his or her anonymity by not bearing any of the personal identification. Maybe allowed fixed percentage of discounts or cumulative to encourage more purchases over time
G
Goods Dispatch Slip - The document which lists what goods and/or services will be or have been sold to your client. It is a main part of inventory management. When in the "posted" state it will ultimately indicate that the listed amount of goods is already sold and is not available in the warehouse and in the non-posted state it may just reserve goods to make sure they will not be sold to anyone else than stated customer or just waiting for the money to come in. You  create one for internal accounting and may print as a packaging slip or a fiscal check.

 

Goods Receipt Slip - A document produced when goods are received into the warehouse. It will usually accompany goods to any inspection and is used to check against invoices before payment.

P
Post/UnPost - To make a document to be accounted for. You may create documents without posting them, so to make a notes for future sales or payments and then as the deal is accepted to post them at once. See also common suggestions and rules of UnPosting the documents
R
Reserve - Any item of goods destined to be sold to particular client should be reserved at the warehouse to be not re-sold or moved or altered any way by the persons who don't know exactly about this deal. This is the main annoyance in place where many sales managers doing their job without knowing the exact, this second state of the warehouse. Reserving eliminates this problem and allowing the multiple simultaneous working seats to carry on their activities without interfering to each other.

The reserving is also used when client orders goods that is not yet paid or client does not guarantee immediate pickup or buying. Then you can make "Client's order" document and reserve the goods if you have plenty of them or they kind bears unique serial numbers etc. So you can dispatch exactly those pieces that had been selected by the client.

V
VAT - Value Added Tax.
