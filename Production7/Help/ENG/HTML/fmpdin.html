Filter panel

 

At the top of the window there is a bar with a few sorting options available to you. You may select a range of dates to display, choose specific business partner or display "All" and narrow down your selection by document status: All, posted or non-posted

Documents table

• icon indicates the document is for incoming payment
• icon indicates the document is Posted
•No. - Document ID number
•Amount in accounting currency - amount paid in document's currency. May be different from document's currency if you deal with overseas customers.
•Amount, USD - actual amount paid, converted into your system default currency (USD in this example).
•Account is your bank account where the funds are being transferred in case of Cashless payment (wire transfer or check deposit)

Document details

When you click on the document in the documents table, the following document details will be displayed in the window below.

•No. - document ID number with last edited date and time
•Business partner - information about payment originator
•Amount - shown in document's currency along with a three-letter currency code
•Status - indicates document status - Posted or Unposted
•Payment type is Cash or Cashless together with receiving bank account number as in this example
•Expenses item - a user-defined category with a brief description of the payment
•Based on - shows reference type and number of the document that was used for this sale (Goods dispatch slip in this case). This is an indication that the payment was received after the goods had been shipped or the service had been provided to a customer.
•Employee - name of the person who created this payment record.

Associated documents list
