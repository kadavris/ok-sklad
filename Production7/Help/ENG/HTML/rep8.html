Business partner report

 Top  Previous  Next

Business partner report contains the information an all transactions (incoming and outgoing) on a selected business partner (supplier or customer). Selection criteria include time period (from date to date) and business partner name. Depending on the nature of business (supplier or customer) the report will show the complete list of products, ID numbers, date of receipt or issue documents, total amounts and final balance with each particular business partner. Positive balance means amount owing to our company, negative balance indicates our debt to this business partner.

You need to start generating this report by clicking on icon on the top toolbar. Alternatively you can use "Ctrl+P" combination or simply double-click on report name. A drop-down menu will allow you to determine report selection criteria. You can specify time period required for the report by clicking on date selection button or enter it manually into date field. If you require a report for all period of your company operation, leave the fields blank.

Then you need to enter business partner's name for which the report is being generated. You may select this name either by using scroll bars or by clicking on icon. Confirm your selection by pressing "Enter" or double-clicking on a selected customer name and it will appear in the "recipient" field. If you need the report on all customers, select "All" from the list.

  When all parameters of this report are specified, you may click on "Preview"  button or icon and the document will be display as it will appear printed.

In order to print the report click on or press  "Ctrl + Р".
