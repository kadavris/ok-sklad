Main properties

Here you define document ID number, source and destination warehouses.

 

•Employee - your name here. If you have administrator privileges for this database, you can select any other employees.
•Issued by - the person responsible for shipping goods from source warehouse
•Received by - the person responsible for receiving of goods

Items table

 

•No. - sequence number
• - goods item
• - indicates that this product item was reserved for transfer.
•Price VAT excluded - net price in your default currency w/o VAT.
•Amount in currency, USD - total for all quantity of goods in currency selected for the document.
•Amount VAT incl. - total for item in you default currency including VAT if applicable
•Serial number - goods serial number(s) of goods if applicable

Individual items editing

First you need to decide if you wish to reserve certain quantity of goods at warehouse.

If you choose not to, you will not be able to post the whole document so goods movement will not be registered.

Type first letters of desired product name under "Name" and the program will offer you the list of closest matches. Using up and down arrows select the product you need. Alternatively you may click on the button on the right to choose the product from the list

This section is for quantity of goods to be transferred.

Quantity - sets the quantity of the product you've selected. Unit of measure depends on the product you choose so it needs to be selected first. Other fields are for reference purposes only.

 

Available at warehouse - displays product quantity available at warehouse.

for - shows total amount in default system currency for given quantity of selected product

If Reserved amount is non zero, it shows total reserved quantity at all warehouses.

If the product selected is traceable by serial numbers, you should enter them here to state which individual pieces of goods to be transferred.
