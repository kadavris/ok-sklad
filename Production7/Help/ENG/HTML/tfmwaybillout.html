Main properties

There are two important controls at the top left corner of the editing window:

Post document - posts the document into the system (the document becomes part of company's financial balance)

Customer - the name of your business partner to whom you sell goods.  You can also enter a desired document number into the "No." field instead of the default sequential number and edit the date and time of actual event if, for example, you wish to register a shipment that was made on a previous day

These items may be left blank.

Complete an authorization - needed for traceability of personnel authorizing shipments and the way the goods were shipped from the warehouse.

Currency  - if necessary you may choose the currency other than your native currency to set and recalculate the prices. In this case the list of items will display the prices in both your native and selected currencies for reference.

Rate - exchange rate for selected currency

This section is used to register payments that have already been issued.
Under "Payment No" enter the number for the payment document, payment type, employee who received the payment in cash if applicable, etc.

Goods processing

These buttons on top of the grid allow you to add or remove goods and services from the list

By clicking on the down arrow of "Add" button you may select the type of goods to add: single or multiple, products or services

Goods list

This table consists of the following columns:

• - goods icon, - service icon
•Reserved - indicates that item was reserved at the warehouse. You can reserve goods even if the whole document is "unposted", to prevent the goods from being sold via another document.
•No. - sequence number
•Product/service - the name of product or service for this item
•Quantity - quantity of products/services to be sold
•Warehouse - name of the warehouse where the goods will be shipped from
•Unit - Unit of measure for this product/service
•Price, VAT included - price in your default currency. If VAT is applicable in your location, displayed price will include it.
•Price excl. VAT - net price in your default currency w/o VAT.
•Price in currency, USD - Price using selected currency for the document. Currency three-letter code will be shown (USD, EUR etc).
•Amount Net - Total for all quantity of goods in you default currency
•Amount in currency, USD - Total for all quantity of goods in the currency selected for the document. Currency three-letter code will be shown (USD, EUR etc).
•Serial number - serial number(s) of goods if applicable

Bottom summary bar

Totals are shown in the currency selected for the document.
