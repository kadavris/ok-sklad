Sales rating

 Top  Previous  Next

This report allows the user to define sales rating per one of the three criteria: sales profits, relative profit or sales volumes.

You need to start generating this report by clicking on icon on the top toolbar. Alternatively you can use "Ctrl+P" combination or simply double-click on report name. A drop-down menu will allow you to determine report selection criteria. You can specify time period required for the report by clicking on date selection button or enter it manually into date field.

Then you need to specify the warehouse for which the report is being prepared. You can either use a scroll bar or click on to view the list of all warehouses in the database. Confirm your selection by pressing "Enter" or double-clicking on required warehouse and it will appear in the "warehouse" field. If you need the report on all warehouses, select "All" from the list.

The next selection criterion is product group. You can either use select it from a drop down menu or click on  icon for the list of all product groups. You are also able to generate a more detailed report on a sub-group by consecutively opening folders with product group names until you encounter the required sub-group. Select product group or sub-group by clicking on "Select" button at bottom right corner of the window or simply press "Enter". If you are interested in a short report on all product groups, you need to select "All" in this field

Then you will need to select one of the available sorting criteria:

a)by quantity
b)by profit margin (product values)
c)by relative profit margin (in %)

 

You can make your selection by clicking on the appropriate radio button at each of the criteria.

Select sorting order in which your data will be represented. You need to select one of the two radio buttons - ascending or descending sorting order. "Include VAT" option will allow you to generate the report on sales rating taking into account a value-added tax.

In the next section you have an option to specify the number of entries in the report - in this case only top entries from the report will be displayed. This will allow you to view either the biggest or smallest figures in the report, depending on your requirements. You can select the number of such entries using a scroll bar or entering it manually in the appropriate field.  

After all report parameters have been specified, you may select "Preview" or click on icon and your document will be displayed on your screen as it will appear printed.

In order to print the report you need to click on icon or press  "Ctrl + Р".
