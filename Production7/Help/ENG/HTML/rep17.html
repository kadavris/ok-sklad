Report on financial results

 Top  Previous  Next

This report represents a combined form containing the information on all operations of goods sales indicating amount of sales, profits received as well as information on all expenses generated by the company -- user of the program. The report also contains dates, document ID numbers, the names of customers and goods recipients. In other words this report combines the report on goods sales with profit and expense report, which makes the final financial report more suitable for further analysis. The first part of report is called "Sales" and contains the information on all operations of product sales for the period specified by the user including total amount and profits.

Second part is called "Expenses" and contains detailed information on all expenses incurred by the company indicating the totals.

Third part "Financial results" shows final result of company's operation for the selected period, which represents the difference between income and actual expenses related to company's activity.

 You need to start generating this report by clicking on icon on the top toolbar. Alternatively you can use "Ctrl+P" combination or simply double-click on report name. A drop-down menu will allow you to determine report selection criteria. You can specify time period required for the report by clicking on date selection button or enter it manually into date field. If you require a report for all period of your company operation, leave the fields blank.

  When all parameters of this report are specified, you may click on "Preview"  button or icon and the document will be display as it will appear printed.

In order to print the report click on or press  "Ctrl + Р".
