Goods Balances Entry

 Top  Previous  Next

This document is necessary when you are just starting work with OK-Inventory, creating a new database or a whole new business.

It is used when you have some goods already available, but don't have the paperwork on hand or don't want to register them in the standard way through Goods receipt slips.

This may also be necessary for occasional purchases from various suppliers that you choose not to include in your database..

Thus the products entered with this document have no record of the original supplier but are processed the same way as regular goods.
