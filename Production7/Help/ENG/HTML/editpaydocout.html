Editing outgoing payments

•Post - toggles posted/unposted status
•No. - internal document ID number
•Payment type - cash or cashless (check, wire, credit card)
•Account - specify bank account information
•Expenses item - select appropriate user-defined payment target description

Here are a few examples of user-defined payment targets.

You can select, add and edit items from this list by clicking the icon on the right.

•Amount - actual amount of money to be paid and currency type.
•Exchange rate - used if non-system default currency is selected.
•Amount in USD - amount of money in system-default currency (USD in this example)
•VAT included - used to simplify VAT inclusion if applicable

•Payer - select from the list of business partners to whom the payment is being made
•Current balance - you may check for some questionable numbers and view full history by clicking the icon on the right
•Employee - originator of the document. Regular users have no permission to change this field, only administrator.

•Pay against document - instantly links this payment with existing goods receipt slip, invoice, etc
•Document type - you need to select your document type first
•No. - enter document ID number manually or click on the icon on the right to select linked document from the list
•Document amount - total amount to be paid according to selected document
•Paid - Amount paid by this customer to date
•Document balance - pending amount to be paid
•Date - date the selected document was created / edited.
