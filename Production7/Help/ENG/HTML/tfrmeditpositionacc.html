This is the main product selection tool.

 

First you need to decide if you wish to reserve certain quantity of goods at the warehouse.

Remember: if you do not reserve items (by default items are not reserved), you won't be able to post the entire document.

Under Search Options you need to enter the attributes of the product.

•Name - search by the beginning of a product name
•Model - search by product model or name (internal or supplier)
•Bar code - search by bar code label. Bar-code scanners are available if you plan on using a Bar code option.

 

Alternatively you may click on button and select  a product from the list

The last field in this section is a list of available warehouses. Click on the down arrow to choose from the list of warehouses where this product is available or click on button to choose from the dialog  window with additional information displayed

This section is for product quantity and pricing.

 

Quantity - enter quantity of the product you selected to include in the document. Enter unit of measure in the next field. Unit of measure depends on the selected product, so select it first. The unit you'll see to the right of the quantity field is for reference only. .

Pricing category used when you want to automatically set the price based on the chosen category settings.

Price incl. VAT - Enter 0 for VAT if it doesn't apply. If you selected some pricing category above, the price will be calculated automatically.

The information on the product quantity available at current warehouse and other locations will be displayed on the right side of this section. If the quantity at current warehouse is not sufficient, you will be able to see if the same product is available at other company warehouses.
The numbers will be shown in red in case the required quantity is not available at selected or any other warehouses.

If Reserved amount is not zero, it indicates the total quantity of this product reserved at all warehouses.

VAT rate - enter % of VAT if applicable. It defaults to global or selected product setting.

In this section you may apply a simple percentage discount or enter customer's discount card number with a preset discount value. The final price and holder of the selected discount card will be displayed for your information.

This section is for information only. It allows you to compare purchasing and selling prices to make sure you keep the required margin.

There is a product manufacturer name and your supplier's tax payer's information if applicable..
