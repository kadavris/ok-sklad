Quick filter bar

 

At the top of the window there is a bar with a few sorting options available to you. You may select a range of dates to display, choose specific business partner or display "All" and narrow down your selection by document status: All, posted or non-posted

Documents list columns

 

The topmost document table columns are:

• - icon of Goods receipt slip document type.
• - indicates that the document has been posted
•No. - Serial number of the document
•Date - date of last save
•Amount, USD - Total goods purchase price in system base currency
•Amount in currency - Total goods purchase price in document's currency
•Paid - indication that this goods have been fully or partially paid by you and there exists a linked outgoing payment(s).
•Document balance - payments pending. Non-zero balance indicates that you are still owing for the listed goods or services

Items Tab

 

When you highlight the document in the main window, the bottom information panel changes according to the data of this slip.

Items tab contains the following information:

 

icon is for goods and icon is for services

•Product - name of product or service under this document.
•Quantity - quantity of product
•Unit - product unit of measure
•Price incl VAT - price per unit (in system default currency) including VAT
•Amount net - total for all quantity of items (in system default currency)
•VAT rate - VAT percentage if applicable
•VAT, USD - VAT markup in document currency (US dollars in this case)
•Amount in currency - Total for all items in document currency (US dollars in this case)
•Serial numbers - Unique serial number of the product, if applicable

 

Details Tab

This is a summary for selected document.

•# - identification number of the document and date when it was last edited
•Amount for the whole document
•Total quantity for all product references
•Based on - if the document is based on a supplier's order
•Received by - person receiver of goods and services stated in the document
•Status - document status - posted or non-posted

 

Associated documents

Displays the names, dates and types of all documents associated with this document, such as outgoing payment(s) for the goods/services received by this document and Goods dispatch slips for the goods received and registered by this documents that was sold.
