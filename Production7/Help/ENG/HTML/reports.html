Reports

This important category helps you to obtain an overall review of data stored in the database. You can see common or detailed information sectioned or sorted for example by time period or business partner or employee. Every report name indicates its purpose, so you can easily identify  the ones you need to get a complete picture of every aspect of your business.

 

Warehouse reports

•Short income report - contains the information on the volume of all incoming goods for any chosen period showing both individual quantities and totals. Selection criteria available here are: date, goods category and warehouse.
•Short sales report - contains the information on sales volume of goods for any chosen period for both individual goods and totals. Selection criteria available here are: date, goods category and warehouse.
•Goods balances log - contains the information on all goods currently available at warehouse (all company warehouses). Selection criteria available here are: date, goods category and warehouse. Besides that goods balances log reflects actual data on the quantity of available and reserved goods from total quantity in stock. Information in this report is represented both as individual and total numbers with calculation of average weighted price per unit of goods.
•Report on goods movement - allows the user to review and print out data on selected goods both received and issued with indication of document ID numbers and dates, names of business partners and current goods balances in stock.

 

•Goods turnover report - this is an expanded report indicating balances of goods in the beginning of the period, goods receipt and issue for selected period, balance of goods at the end of period including average weighted prices. Selection criteria include time periods, goods categories and warehouse name. The process for report generating and printing out is similar to the report on goods movement.
•Sales rating - allows you to determine goods sales rating on one of the following criteria: profit levels, relative profits and sales volumes.

 

Financial reports

•Sales report with profits - contains the information on all financial transactions of goods sales indicating the profits received but without VAT. Selection criteria include time periods (from date to date), goods categories and company warehouses.
•Sales dynamics - contains the information on sales dynamics for the selected period, represented in the form of a diagram.

 

Business partners

•Income reports on suppliers - contains the information on all incoming documents received from suppliers indicating document issue date, document ID numbers, product range and price of goods received from suppliers. Selection criteria include time periods (from date to date) and supplier name.
•Lenders list - this report contains the information on all lenders indicating the amount owing to these business partners. Selection criteria are determined as time period (from date to date).
•Debtors list - this report contains the information on all debtors indicating the amount owing to our company indicating the amount owed. Selection criteria are determined as time period (from date to date).
•Business partner report - this report contains the information on all operations (goods receipt and issue) for the selected business partner. Selection criteria are time period (from date to date) and business partner name. Depending on business partner type (supplier or customer), the report will contain complete list of goods, ID numbers, dates of goods receipt and goods dispatch slips, total amounts as well as financial balances with this business partner. Positive number (+) indicates that our company is owing to this business partner and negative balance (-) reflects the amount owned to our company.

 

Documents

•Document register - this report contains the information on all operations performed by the company indicating ID numbers, dates, totals of the document and business partner names. Selection criteria available here are time periods and document types.
