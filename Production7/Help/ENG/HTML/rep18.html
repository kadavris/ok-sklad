Report on minimal goods balances

 Top  Previous  Next

This report contains the information on goods at warehouse (all warehouse of the company -- user of the program), for which the available balances are less than minimal. Reminder: size of minimal goods balances is set by the user when the product card is first filled out. If the amount of goods is currently less than minimal for normal operation of the company, it will be automatically included in this report. The amount of goods that needs to be purchased will be indicated under column "Required". Selection criteria available for this report include the name of warehouse and goods groups.

You need to start generating this report by clicking on icon on the top toolbar. Alternatively you can use "Ctrl+P" combination or simply double-click on report name. A drop-down menu will allow you to determine report selection criteria. You can specify time period required for the report by clicking on date selection button or enter it manually into date field.

Then you need to specify the warehouse for which the report is being prepared. You can either use a scroll bar or click on to view the list of all warehouses in the database. Confirm your selection by pressing "Enter" or double-clicking on required warehouse and it will appear in the "warehouse" field. If you need the report on all warehouses, select "All" from the list.

The next selection criterion is product group. You can either use select it from a drop down menu or click on  icon for the list of all product groups. You are also able to generate a more detailed report on a sub-group by consecutively opening folders with product group names until you encounter the required sub-group. Alternatively, you may click on

"View products from sub-groups". In this case from any product groups you will have access to products in the sub-groups belonging to the selected group.

Select product group or sub-group by clicking on "Select" button at bottom right corner of the window or simply press "Enter". If you are interested in a short report on all product groups, you need to select "All" in this field

Group by product groups . A check mark in this box will set or cancel forming of the report by product groups. In case you choose to leave this box blank, the report will be generated with the products in an alphabetical order.

When all parameters of this report are specified, you may click on "Preview"  button or icon and the document will be display as it will appear printed.

In order to print the report click on or press  "Ctrl + Р".
