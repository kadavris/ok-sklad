Expense reports

 Top  Previous  Next

This report contains the information on all expenses incurred by the company -- program user indicating the dates, document ID numbers and customer names. Selection criteria include time periods (from date to date) and customer name (legal entity or individuals) to whom the payments for goods and services have been made.

  You need to start generating this report by clicking on icon on the top toolbar. Alternatively you can use "Ctrl+P" combination or simply double-click on report name. A drop-down menu will allow you to determine report selection criteria. You can specify time period required for the report by clicking on date selection button or enter it manually into date field. If you require a report for all period of your company operation, leave the fields blank.

Specify recipient name on which the report is being generated. You can select a recipient using a scroll bar or by clicking on icon. The recipient may be both legal entity or individual, company employee. Depending on the category of recipient, he may be found under appropriate category: companies, individuals or company employees).

 

Confirm your selection by pressing "Enter" or double-clicking on a selected recipient name and it will appear in the "recipient" field. If you need the report on all recipients, select "All" from the list.

In the field "Expense item" indicate expense category (expense type). For example, your expenses may include rent payments for offices and warehouses, electricity, stationery, hardware, office furniture, personnel salaries etc. If you wish to obtain a report on all expense categories, enter "All" in the "Expense item" field

  When all parameters of this report are specified, you may click on "Preview"  button or icon and the document will be display as it will appear printed.

In order to print the report click on or press  "Ctrl + Р".
